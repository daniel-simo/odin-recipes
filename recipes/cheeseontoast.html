The Perfect Cheese on Toast

Description

A quick and easy snap the easy your hunger can be used any time of the day. Leaving you satisfied and cheesed up.

Ingredients

  • As many slices of bread as you want
  • Enough cheese to cover the bread
  • Tub of butter
  • Herbs if you are feeling fancy

Steps

  1. Put bread under the grill.
  2. Slice up the cheese.
  3. Once bread started to toast take out, butter and add cheese and herbs.
  4. Once cheese has melted remove from grill slice up, done.
